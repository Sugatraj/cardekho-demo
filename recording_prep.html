Trial Prep & Recording Checklist

Ensure a flawless unedited screen recording of your 2-3 hour build. Use this guide to set up, test, and prepare your environment before starting the timer.

The Mission & Target Dataset

Build a full-stack tool that helps car buyers filter, search, compare, or query the cardekho_dataset.csv (15k+ used cars, features like price, km driven, mileage, power, transmission, etc.). The goal is to move a buyer from "I don't know what to buy" to "I'm confident in my shortlist."

15.4k Cars Full-Stack (Front + Back) 2-3 Hours Boxed Screen Record Mandatory (Most Evaluated)
1

Prepare Recording Hardware & Software

Recorder Settings

  • Use OBS Studio, Loom, or Zoom / QuickTime.
  • Set screen capture to Entire Desktop (not just the IDE). The reviewers want to see the terminal, IDE, and browser side-by-side.
  • Ensure resolution is legible (1080p recommended). If using a 4K monitor, scale UI elements up so text is clearly readable.

Audio & Privacy Check

  • Enable your Microphone. Talking through your decisions can dramatically boost the "Agentic Tool Usage" evaluation.
  • Close personal chats (Slack, WhatsApp, Discord, Email).
  • Turn on Do Not Disturb / Focus Mode to block push notifications during the session.
2

Establish the Trial Demo Environment

Before launching the real recording, perform a 10-minute trial demo to guarantee system stability and test your setup.

Step 2.1

Create Sandbox Directory

Initialize a temporary folder separate from your final workspace. Call it trial-demo.

Step 2.2

Verify AI Tool Integrations

Verify your API keys, LLM credentials (e.g., Claude, OpenAI), or editor configurations (e.g. Cursor, Copilot) are active and functional.

Step 2.3

Perform Dry Run

Record yourself for 2 minutes: write a small function, run a local terminal command, and verify playback has clear audio and crisp video.

3

Prepare Final Workspace Architecture

Decide your tech stack and skeleton directory before clicking record. A rapid, low-friction framework is essential to complete the project in 2-3 hours.
